1. When You Can Cancel

  • Orders can be cancelled directly in the Scooty app before the restaurant/vendor accepts them.
  • Once preparation or dispatch has started, cancellation may not be possible. If allowed, only a partial refund may apply.
  • For scheduled deliveries or bookings (e.g., events, travel), cancellations must be made within the specified notice period (usually 24–48 hours) to qualify for a refund.

2. Refund Eligibility

Refunds may be requested in the following situations:

  • Orders cancelled before preparation or dispatch.
  • Orders not delivered or delivered more than 30 minutes after ETA.
  • Orders with missing, incorrect, or damaged items.
  • Payments processed in error (e.g., duplicate charges).

3. Refund Destination

  • Refunds will be credited back to your original payment method (card, bank transfer, wallet).
  • If the original method is unavailable, Scooty will issue the refund to your Scooty Wallet, which can be used for future orders or withdrawn to a bank account upon request.
  • Instant Wallet Credits: For minor issues (e.g., missing side items), Scooty will issue immediate wallet credits while processing the full refund separately.

4. Automatic Compensation

  • For undelivered orders, Scooty will automatically issue a refund without requiring a claim.
  • For late deliveries, Scooty will apply the Late Delivery Compensation Framework (wallet credits + loyalty points).

5. How to Request a Refund or Cancel

  • Open the Scooty app → go to the Orders tab → select your order → tap Cancel Order.
  • For scheduled services, contact Scooty Support through the app.
  • Refund requests must be submitted within 7 days of the issue.
  • Supporting evidence (photos, order details, receipts) may be required for claims involving incorrect or damaged items.

6. Refund Timing

  • Requests are acknowledged within 24 hours.
  • Refunds are processed within 3–5 business days, depending on your bank or payment provider.

7. Important Notes

  • Promo codes and discounts are not refundable. However, Scooty may issue goodwill loyalty points if promo credits were applied to a failed order.
  • Refunds will not apply if delivery fails due to incorrect addresses or customer unavailability.
  • For travel/event bookings, tiered cancellation rules may apply (e.g., partial refunds if cancelled well in advance, no refunds close to the date).

8. Transparency & Goodwill

Scooty commits to handling refunds and cancellations fairly, promptly, and transparently. Customers affected by refund delays will receive bonus loyalty points as a goodwill gesture.
