Access Control & Detection

Access Control

Access control systems replace metal keys with key cards, fobs, electronic passcodes and monitored electronic locks. Owners can specify who enters specific areas and at what times.

Key Benefits
  • Prevents unauthorised key duplication
  • Electronic locks cannot be picked
  • Central dashboard for monitoring
  • Enhanced confidentiality

Intercom Systems

Communication between rooms or locations via network connections. Indoor and outdoor models available, powered by PoE.

IP Features

  • High quality speaker/mic
  • Vol/Monitor/PTT controls
  • Active/Link/Talk LEDs
  • TalkMaster Solo Software
  • PoE & 3.5mm Jacks

Outdoor Models

  • TVIP-intercomPA-O-2G: 1 watt, PoE, flush-mounted stainless steel.
  • TVIP-intercomPA-O-BB: 1 watt, PoE, surface-mounted with hood.
  • TVIP-IntercomPA: Outdoor enclosure, network module, analog panel.

Use Cases

  • Network Attached Manual door access control. Standalone or integrated with cameras/access systems.
  • Apartments Multi-tenant systems with built-in cameras, mobile-enabled access, and lock integration.
